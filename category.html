Catering

  • Full service

    With full service, we handle every aspect of your meal for you — even the dreaded cleanup. From a personalized menu based on the style of your event, to food that is fully prepared onsite and served fresh to your guests. You won't need to worry about anything — we'll make sure you and your guests receive only the finest food, service, and personal attention.

  • Delivery

    With delivery catering you get gourmet quality food delivered straight from our kitchen to your event — whether it's taking place at your home, office, or a special location. Our simplified service includes a variety of à la carte year-round and seasonal menus to choose from, along with upscale trays and platters so you never have to compromise on quality or presentation.
